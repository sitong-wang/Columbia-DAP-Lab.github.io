DAPLab

AI Agents for Work

Columbia University | March 12, 2025

How can AI Agents streamline work and operations?

This workshop brings together industry and academic experts at Columbia University to share the latest innovations and challenges for how AI agents can streamline work and operations.

This workshop is a product of Columbia University's Data-Agents-Process Lab - a collection of AI, ML, Operations and Systems researchers dedicated to addressing the most pressing challenges in unlocking the potential of AI agents for work.

When: March 12, 2025

Where: Columbia Business School, Kravis Hall

Time: 9:30-5pm, followed by a happy hour mixer
